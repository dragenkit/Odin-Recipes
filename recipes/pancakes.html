Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 473ml milk
  • 94g flour
  • 1 Tbsp. sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 Tbsp. melted butter

Steps

  1. mix all ingredients in a bowl
  2. heat up a pan with oil in it
  3. pour your batter in the pan and flip
    the pancake when bubbles form on top
